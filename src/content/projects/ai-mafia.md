AI Mafia is the strongest public signal on the site: a visual artifact about AI history, founder networks, research lineage, and the culture of modern technology.

The project was inspired by the Acquired Google story and shaped as an explorable map rather than a flat essay. The point is not just to show connections, but to make the structure behind an era easier to inspect.

It belongs at the front of the work section because it combines taste, technical craft, AI interest, and business storytelling.
